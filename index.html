Welcome!

I am Ionuț Muntean, a professor at the Automation Department of the Technical University of Cluj-Napoca (UTCN), Romania, where I connect academic research with real-world technological challenges.

With years of experience managing multidisciplinary teams, I guide complex projects in mobility, IoT, robotics, and automation. I focus on mentoring students and young engineers by involving them in strategic partnerships.

My expertise includes innovation portfolio management, project lifecycle supervision, and the coordination of complex R&D projects in emerging technology domains, such as AI, microelectronics, hydrogen, and battery management .

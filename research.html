Research Areas

My research activity focuses on the design and implementation of intelligent embedded systems, autonomous robotic platforms, and distributed architectures for real-time data acquisition and control.

Key domains:

  • End to end AI with applications in mobility
  • Autonomous robotics and perception
  • Embedded and Cyber-Physical Systems
  • Localization, Mapping, and Sensor Fusion
  • Modelling, simulation and control of chemical processes
